About buildCent

buildCent is the nCent Labs incentive protocol that incentivizes workers to collaborate on building a project. buildCent aims to bring together the most skilled workers to build the best projects possible.

How it Works

With the buildCent system, every project has a sponsor and collaborators. Each transaction using buildCent system consists of two payments, an NCNT payment that serves as a "downpayment" for the work to be done and a payment in buildCents. The payment in buildCents encompasses the bulk of the compensation for the work
done plus a bonus for collaboration.

Below is an example chain of a project completed with buildCent.

Ana, the Sponsor

Ana wants to build a car and has 1,000 NCNT. She stamps 100 of them as buildCents. She builds most of the car but knows people who make better car doors and engines than she can. She passes 30 buildCent and 100 NCNT to Bob, who makes the best car doors. She also passes 300 NCNT and 70 buildCent to Carl, who makes the best car engines.

Bob

Bob is confident he can make the best car door, but he knows that Daniela specializes in making car door handles. To make the best door possible, Bob passes Daniela 20 NCNT and 10 buildCent.

Carl

Carl builds the engine and reports back to Ana. She approves of its quality and redeems his buildCents.

Daniela

Daniela makes the car's door handle and shows it to Bob. Bob thinks it's an excellent handle, so he signs off that Daniela has finished her task. She must wait until Bob finishes the door to redeem her buildCent.

Bob

Bob puts together his car door with Daniela's handle. She shows it to Ana, who is really happy with the final product.

Ana

Ana is really satisfied with the car door, so she redeems Bob and Ana's buildCents.

The Result

By incentivizing collaboration, Ana was able to build a much better car than she could have built on her own. And, during the process, everyone involved received rewards for helping her finish this project in addition to the payment for the task.

Reputation

Build a reputation with buildCent. Your sponsors and collaborators will rate the quality of your work. Sponsors will see how amazing you are.

Rewards for Referrals

One key differentiator about buildCent is that we reward referrals. If you're caught up with your own projects, passing buildCents on to someone who can complete the task you were given will still result in a reward.
